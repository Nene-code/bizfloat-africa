What's Inside the App

Five pillars that work together like a full financial team

A personal financial advisor, business analyst, and market intelligence system, available on any mobile phone, at no professional consultation fee.

Pillar 1

Financial Tracking & Credit Building

A simple mobile interface for logging daily sales and expenses. Over time, an AI engine processes this activity into a Business Health Score, a credible, data-driven financial profile built entirely from the user's own business behavior. No bank account or formal records required. This score becomes the foundation for accessing credit through partner microfinance institutions and fintech lenders, directly inside the app.

Pillar 2

Voice-Based Transaction Recording

Many business owners have limited time, low literacy, or simply find typing inconvenient. Speaking sales and expenses aloud in a local language lets the app automatically capture, transcribe, and categorize the transaction, removing one of the biggest barriers to consistent recordkeeping.

Pillar 3

Local Language Support

BizFloat Africa runs fully in French, English, Pidgin, Hausa, Swahili, and Yoruba, covering all five target markets. Every screen, prompt, lesson, and AI response is available in the user's preferred language, built into the core of the app rather than bolted on as a translation layer.

Pillar 4

AI Prediction & Advisory

Beyond scoring, the AI analyzes historical data to forecast future performance, flagging slow seasons, predicting cash flow gaps, and recommending the best windows to restock, save, or invest. A Market Intelligence Feed translates broader economic trends into plain-language guidance.

Pillar 5

Digital Literacy Hub

Short, visual, multilingual courses on mobile money, digital recordkeeping, online selling, cybersecurity basics, and using technology to grow a business, designed for users with low literacy and minimal prior tech experience.

Bonus Feature

AI Business Coach

A conversational AI assistant embedded throughout the app. Owners ask financial questions in plain language, by typing or by voice, and get guidance tailored to their specific business context, in any of the five target markets.
