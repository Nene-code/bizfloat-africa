The Four Barriers

Small businesses don't stay small by choice

Financial invisibility, no access to credit, low digital literacy, and no market awareness compound each other, trapping capable business owners at the margins of the formal economy, year after year.

Financial Invisibility

No bank statements or credit history means years of honest work count for nothing on paper.

No Access to Credit

Locked out of formal lending, owners fall back on exploitative informal lenders or savings alone.

Low Digital Literacy

Mobile banking and digital tools remain intimidating and out of reach for the people who need them most.

No Market Awareness

Pricing, restocking, and saving decisions run on intuition, with no visibility into trends or risks.

Pa Ekema has run the same convenience store for over thirty years. Same size, same stock, same hand-written receipts. He isn't lazy, and his business isn't failing. It simply never had access to the tools, capital, or knowledge to grow.

His story repeats itself across millions of small businesses on the continent. BizFloat Africa exists to change that pattern, one Business Health Score at a time.

The Real Cost of Exclusion

One store, one generation, one cycle that never got to break

Micro and small businesses are the backbone of African economies, yet the systems built to support growth were never designed with them in mind. BizFloat Africa builds the missing bridge between everyday business activity and formal financial opportunity.

Five Pillars, One App

Everything a growing business needs, in one place

Pillar 1

Financial Tracking & Credit Building

Log daily sales and expenses in a simple interface. The AI turns that behavior into a Business Health Score lenders can trust.

Pillar 2

Voice-Based Recording

Speak sales and expenses aloud in your own language, no typing, no literacy barrier, just a quick word into the phone.

Pillar 3

Local Language Support

French, English, Pidgin, Hausa, Swahili, and Yoruba, built in from onboarding to the AI coach, in all five markets.

Pillar 4

AI Prediction & Advisory

Forecasts cash flow gaps, flags slow seasons, and recommends the best time to restock, save, or invest.

Pillar 5

Digital Literacy Hub

Short, visual lessons on mobile money, digital recordkeeping, online selling, and cybersecurity basics.

Bonus

AI Business Coach

Ask financial questions by typing or speaking, get answers tailored to your business, in your language.
