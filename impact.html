Why This Matters

Financial exclusion isn't an accident of effort. It's a design gap.

Micro and small businesses make up the largest segment of Africa's workforce and drive real GDP — yet most remain locked out of the formal financial system, not because their businesses fail, but because the system was never built to see them.

Financial Invisibility

No bank statements, no credit history — years of profitable operation count for nothing in a paper-only system.

No Access to Credit

Owners fall back on high-interest informal lenders or personal savings — exploitative or insufficient either way.

Low Digital Literacy

Mobile banking and digital payments remain inaccessible to the very people who stand to benefit most.

No Market Awareness

Pricing, restocking, and investment decisions run on intuition — vulnerable to risks that could've been anticipated.

Sustainable Development Goals

Three SDGs, one platform

BizFloat Africa directly advances three Sustainable Development Goals that sit at the heart of everything the platform is built to do.

Primary · SDG 8
08

Decent Work & Economic Growth

Through the AI Trend Predictor, Market Intelligence Feed, and cash flow tracking, owners plan ahead and grow with intention rather than guesswork — creating more stable employment at scale.

Secondary · SDG 10
10

Reduced Inequalities

Replacing traditional credit requirements with an AI-generated Business Health Score creates a new pathway into the financial system — no paperwork, collateral, or bank account required.

Tertiary · SDG 9
09

Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

AI-powered financial infrastructure — voice recording, prediction, market intelligence — becomes available on any mobile phone, extending digital transformation to businesses long left out of it.
