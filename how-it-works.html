The Journey

From a spoken sale to a real credit offer

This is the actual path a business owner takes inside the app, no paperwork, no branch visit, no waiting on a loan officer.

1

Log a sale, by voice or by typing

Say "Sold five bags of rice for 12,000 naira" in your own language, or type it in. BizFloat transcribes and categorizes it automatically.

2

The AI reads the pattern over time

Daily sales, expenses, savings, and spending patterns build up into a behavioral profile, no bank account or paper records needed.

3

A Business Health Score is generated

The score classifies the business as Healthy, Moderate, or High Risk, and updates as new transactions come in.

4

Real credit offers appear in-app

Partner microfinance institutions and fintech lenders surface offers matched to the score, reviewed and accepted without leaving BizFloat.

Along the Way

The app never stops coaching

Two things run quietly in the background of every step above, ready whenever the owner needs them.

Ask the AI Business Coach anything

"Should I restock now or wait?" "Why did my score drop this week?" The coach answers in plain language, by voice or text, in the owner's own language.

Pick up a short lesson in the Literacy Hub

Between transactions, a two-minute lesson on mobile money or digital recordkeeping keeps building the skills behind the score.
